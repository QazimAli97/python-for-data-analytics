# 🐼 Day 18 – GroupBy & Aggregation in Pandas

## 📌 Introduction
Day 18 focuses on one of the most powerful operations in data analytics: **GroupBy and Aggregation**.

GroupBy allows analysts to summarize large datasets and calculate business metrics such as totals, averages, and counts.

This is the foundation of KPI analysis.

---

## 🎯 Objective
The goal of Day 18 is to:

- Group data by categories
- Calculate totals and averages
- Perform multiple aggregations
- Generate summarized reports
- Extract business insights from datasets

---

## 🧠 Why GroupBy Matters in Data Analytics

In real-world analytics workflows:

- Sales are summarized by product
- Revenue is analyzed by region
- Customer purchases are grouped by category
- KPIs are calculated using aggregation

GroupBy transforms raw data into meaningful summaries.

---

## 🔎 Concepts Covered

- `groupby()` function
- Aggregation functions (`sum`, `mean`)
- Grouping data by categories
- Multiple aggregations using `agg()`
- Generating summarized reports

The script demonstrates:

## 💡 Key Learning

GroupBy operations allow analysts to transform large datasets into meaningful summaries.

Aggregation converts raw data into insights.
