# 🐼 Day 17 – Filtering & Selecting Data in Pandas

## 📌 Introduction
Day 17 focuses on selecting and filtering data using Pandas.

Filtering is one of the most important skills in data analytics because insights come from narrowing down large datasets into meaningful subsets.

---

## 🎯 Objective
The goal of Day 17 is to:

- Select specific columns
- Filter rows based on conditions
- Apply multiple conditions
- Use logical operators in Pandas
- Extract meaningful business insights

---

## 🧠 Why Filtering Matters in Data Analytics

In real-world analytics workflows:

- Identifying high-value customers
- Filtering profitable transactions
- Detecting underperforming products
- Segmenting data based on criteria
- Applying business rules

Raw data becomes valuable only after filtering.

---

## 🔎 Concepts Covered

- Selecting single columns: `df["column"]`
- Selecting multiple columns: `df[["col1", "col2"]]`
- Conditional filtering
- Logical operators: `&` (AND), `|` (OR)
- Creating new calculated columns

---

## 🧪 Script Summary

The script demonstrates:

- Creating a sales dataset
- Adding a Revenue column
- Selecting specific columns
- Filtering high-revenue orders
- Applying multiple conditions

This simulates real-world sales performance analysis.

---

## 💡 Key Learning

Filtering transforms raw datasets into actionable insights.

Mastering filtering prepares the foundation for:

- GroupBy operations
- KPI analysis
- Customer segmentation
- Business intelligence reporting

Data analysis is not about all data.
It’s about the right data.
